I am a fifth year Ph.D. student in the Stanford Statistics Department advised by Prof. John Duchi. My research mainly centers around Conformal Inference, Distributional Robustness and Causal Inference. I am interested in developing methods to assess reliability of predictions and replicability of statistical findings mainly under distributional shifts.

Prior to joining Stanford, I completed my Bachelor's and Master's degree in Statistics from Indian Statistical Institute (ISI) in 2017.
